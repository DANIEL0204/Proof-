Frequently asked questions.

What is Proof and how does it work?

Proof is a cutting-edge fintech platform that simplifies financial management. It works by securely aggregating all your financial accounts and transactions in one place, allowing you to track your income, expenses, investments, and debts effortlessly.

Is my financial data safe with Proof?

Absolutely. At Proof, the security of your financial data is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to safeguard your information. Plus, we never store your login credentials, ensuring your data remains protected.

Can I connect multiple bank accounts and credit cards to Proof?

Yes, you can connect all your financial accounts to Proof. Our platform supports connections to various banks, credit cards, investment accounts, and more. This enables you to have a comprehensive view of your financial life.

Does Proof offer budgeting and financial planning tools?

Yes, Proof provides powerful budgeting and financial planning tools. You can set budget goals, track your spending, and receive insights into your financial health. Our platform also offers personalized recommendations to help you achieve your financial goals.

What fees are associated with using Proof?

Proof offers both free and premium subscription plans. The free plan includes basic features, while the premium plan offers advanced tools and priority support. You can find detailed information about pricing on our Pricing page.

How can I get in touch with Proof's customer support?

Our dedicated customer support team is available to assist you. You can reach us through the Contact Us page on our website. We're here to answer your questions, address concerns, and provide the support you need.
